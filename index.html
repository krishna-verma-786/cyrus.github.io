Cllr. Pape Suah is a lawyer and has practiced for many years and currently serves as the Legal Counsel for Afriland First Bank in Liberia. He is an active member of the Liberia National Bar Association (LNBA). He studied at the University of Liberia where he received his bachelor and later enrolled and graduated from the Louis Arthur Grimes School of Law and had attended several trainings that have prepared him to render service to his community and Country, Liberia


During the last few decades, law firms themselves have turned into businesses. Overhead has grown. Law firms have merged and then merged again, creating ever-larger law firms. The focus on profits per partner overshadowed the relationship between the client and its attorney. The further attorneys and their firms moved from engaging in law as a profession, the harder it became for clients to find attorneys who were real business partners. Indeed, there was a better way.
